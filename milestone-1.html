Purpose, Audience, Goal

Purpose: This project will be a professional portfolio to showcase my front end web development skills and experience.

Audience: This online portfolio will reach a global professional audience including recruiters, startup founders, hiring managers, and clients.

Goal: The goal of this portfolio is to allow the target audience to learn about the skill set I have and collaborate about employment opportunities.

Persona

Name:Tiegan Ferris

Age:38

Location: San Francisco, California

Profession: Founder of MySkin skincare line, a startup/e-commerce shop that is looking to expand business, and wants to hire a full-time, in-house Front-End web Developer. They just received a grant that will fund this position among others.

Technology: User is using a 21" mac desktop with operating system mac OS Big Sur version 11.6. An Iphone 12 Pro with 5G data. User uses HR recruiting desktop applications.

Attitudes and Behaviors: User is always juggling hiring managers and canidates and trys to accomodate both. User has a full schedule that consists of phone screening appointments and some virtual Zoom meetings. User spends time seeking potential canidates on Linkedin, Handshake, and through networking events. User screens resumes and portfolios for no more than 5 minutes total.

Frustrations and Needs:Tiegan is feeling overwhelmed with the quick growth of her new skincare company and has to use the grant money accordingly. She is adding new skincare products to her line and needs to keep her website updated and functioning optimally. She is wanting to hire an entry level bootcamp graduate. She is looking for someone who is passionate about wellness and self-care. She is looking to hire someone within two weeks.

Goals:Tiegan is concerned about finding a quality candidate quickly to fill the Web Development position. She wants to find someone quickly to utilize the grant money and keep the startup growth continuing.

User Story:

As a user, I need to find a suitable candidate for MySkin and is a competent mid-level Front End Web Developer to maintain the website.
