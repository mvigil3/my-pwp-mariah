Purpose, Audience, Goal

Purpose: This project will be a professional portfolio to showcase my full stack web development skills and experience.

Audience: This online portfolio will reach a global professional audience including recruiters, startup founders, hiring managers, and clients.

Goal: The goal of this portfolio is to allow the target audience to learn about the web development skill set I have and collaborate about employment opportunities. I will have contact information and a link to my Linkedin so that others can connect with me.

Persona

Name:Tiegan Ferris

Age:38

Location: San Francisco, California

Profession: Founder of MySkin skincare line, a startup/e-commerce shop that is looking to expand business, and wants to hire a full-time, in-house Front-End web Developer. They just received a grant that will fund this position and allow more products to be added to the skincare line.

Technology: User is using a 21" mac desktop with operating system Mac OS Big Sur version 11.6. User has an Iphone 12 Pro with 5G data. User uses HR recruiting desktop applications and Slack to communicates with colleagues internally.

Attitudes and Behaviors: User is overwhelmed since she has been juggling every aspect of her startup. User is extremely eager to delegate work in her startup. She currently has a full schedule filled with Zoom meetings and phone calls. User spends time seeking potential canidates on Linkedin, Handshake, and through networking events. User screens resumes and portfolios for no more than 5 minutes total. She would like to keep phone screening to no more than 15 minutes.

Frustrations and Needs:Tiegan is feeling overwhelmed with the quick growth of her new skincare company but is feeling grateful. She is happy that she was awarded this grant to grow her startup and one of her priorities is finding a web developer since it is an ecommerce business. She is adding new skincare products to her line and needs to keep her website updated and functioning optimally. She is wanting to hire an entry level bootcamp graduate. She is looking for someone who is passionate about wellness and self-care. She is looking to hire someone within two weeks.

Goals: Tiegan is concerned about finding a quality candidate quickly to fill the Web Development position. She wants to make sure they are proactive and can keep the website functioning for consumers. She wants to find someone quickly to utilize the grant money and keep the startup growth progressing.

User Story: As a user, I need to quickly find a suitable candidate for MySkin who is a competent mid-level Front End Web Developer to maintain the website and needs minimal supervison.
